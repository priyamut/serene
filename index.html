Meet The Dentist

Gentle, Fair and Friendly – These are ways in which Dr. Sapana Majmudar is often described.

She is an absolute believer of conservative treatment and she always puts the patients first. Whether your need is implants to replace missing teeth, veneers to improve your smile, teeth whitening, a root canal or anything else, Dr. Majmudar’s experience and care will help guide you through the process. She sees patients as young as two. Dr. Sapana has been practicing dentistry since 1992. Dr. Sapana Majmudar is a member of the American Dental Association and California Dental Association. She is happily married and has two beautiful children. She enjoys hiking, crafting, cooking and traveling.

When you visit our Fremont dental office, your smile is our top priority. Dr. Sapana Majmudar & her entire team are dedicated to providing you with the personalized, gentle care that you deserve, as well as the very best in customer service to make you smile throughout every aspect of your dental experience. By utilizing the best technology combined with the latest techniques and materials, you will experience the BEST dental care possible. Some of our most advanced technology includes 3D xrays for precise dental work, Carivue Cavity Detection to detect new and developing cavities without radiation, high definition intra-oral cameras so patients can be educated about their conditions, laser technology for gums treatment, ICON treatment for treating small cavities without drilling, and SDF (sodium diamine fluoride) treatment to stop cavities for kids.

From the minute you enter our office you will truly feel like family and we try to make each patient as comfortable as possible. We offer many things for patients’ comfort including beverages, blankets to make you feel cozy, and movies to watch during treatment so you can divert your mind. Good patients who maintain their cleaning schedule may even win free movie tickets. You owe it to yourself to experience the best dental practice in Fremont!
